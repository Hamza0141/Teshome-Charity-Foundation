About Us

  • Home

Welcome to the Teshome Charity Foundation (TCF), a compassionate non-profit organization committed to rebuilding hope and opportunities in the regions highly affected by conflict and drought. Through our unwavering dedication, we aim to support individuals and families by reconstructing damaged Health Care and Educational facilities and providing necessary resources, we strive to empower communities to rebuild and thrive despite the challenges they face. Join us in our journey to make a meaningful difference in the lives of Ethiopians. Together, we can restore hope, rebuild communities, and create a brighter future for all.

Join Us
 

Our Mission

 

At TCF, our mission is crystal clear: to reopen vital healthcare and educational services that have been disrupted by both human conflicts and natural disasters. The prolonged political turmoil in Ethiopia has plunged the nation into continuous warfare, resulting in immense suffering for innocent civilians. In response, TCF has outlined three primary areas of focus to address immediate needs:

Restoring Facilities and Supplies

Reestablishing health facilities in affected areas and equipping them with essential medical supplies.

Supporting Dedicated Personnel Amid Crisis

Providing encouragement and support to the dedicated personnel working tirelessly to maintain services.

Ensuring Access to Education

Supporting the reopening of schools to ensure children have access to education
