Our Mission

 

At TCF, our mission is crystal clear: to reopen vital healthcare and educational services that have been disrupted by both human conflicts and natural disasters. The prolonged political turmoil in Ethiopia has plunged the nation into continuous warfare, resulting in immense suffering for innocent civilians. In response, TCF has outlined three primary areas of focus to address immediate needs:

Restoring Facilities and Supplies

Reestablishing health facilities in affected areas and equipping them with essential medical supplies.

Supporting Dedicated Personnel Amid Crisis

Providing encouragement and support to the dedicated personnel working tirelessly to maintain services in devastated health and education facilities due to the war.

Ensuring Access to Education

Supporting the reopening of schools to ensure children have access to education
